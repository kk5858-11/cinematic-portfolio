The best digital products feel like environments, not documents. They breathe.

When we talk about cinematic web design, we mean slow easing, intentional blur, and typography that owns the viewport — not particles and neon gradients.

Motion should answer a question: where should the eye travel next? If animation does not clarify hierarchy, it is noise.

This site is an experiment in that philosophy: monochrome, deep space, and scroll as a camera move.
