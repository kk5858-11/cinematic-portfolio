WebGL is not a fireworks show. Used well, it is lighting and depth — aurora, fog, particles with weight.

Three rules: degrade gracefully, lazy load the canvas, and never block reading the message.

The hero on this portfolio follows those rules. If your device prefers reduced motion or cannot render WebGL, you still get the same mood through CSS.

That is not a fallback. That is part of the design system.
